6 Module Clover Kasudama

I've do some 6 module clover kasudama model. The final model is not very stable but it is standing. The cool thing about this model is that when you're making 30 mudule model, out of 5 cm paper squares, which are cut ou of 15 cm paper, you can fold those six modules from the remaining paper.
